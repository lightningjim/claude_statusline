<objective>
Wire the Plan 01 classification/color/selection layer into the live trailing-detail render path. Replace the single warning glyph with a per-class glyph, color the primary alert by class hue + intensity, sanitize the NWS event text, and render the remainder as a per-class tally.

Purpose: This is where the meteorologist sees the payoff — the bar's trailing alert detail now tells you the action level (Warning/Watch/Advisory) by hue, the immediacy by brightness, the kind by glyph, and the full remaining hazard picture by per-class tally — all within the top line's unguaranteed width.
Output: Updated `_weather_segment` alert-override block + new `_build_alert_tally` helper in `claude-statusline.py`, with override/E2E tests updated and green.
</objective>

renders out-of-the-box (D-07). Config supplies per-segment toggles

(D-09). A toggled-off segment is not rendered (D-08).

Purpose: Makes the bar configurable per CFG-01 without ever breaking the out-of-the-box experience.
Output: ~/.claude/claude-statusline.py with a config loader and toggle/threshold wiring;
~/.claude/claude-statusline.toml with documented defaults. Covers CFG-01.

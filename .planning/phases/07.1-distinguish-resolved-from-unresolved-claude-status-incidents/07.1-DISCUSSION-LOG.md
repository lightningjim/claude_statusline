  intersection, newest resolved incident wins) — offered, left as discretion.

## Deferred Ideas

- Fixed time-window lifecycle (rejected in favor of component-state lifecycle, D-01).
- Encoding past severity in the resolved look (rejected for uniform GREEN, D-03).
- Resolved-degraded outranking maintenance (rejected to keep Rule-3 order, D-04).
- Resolved-green for a muted incident (rejected; muting wins, D-06).
- Incident-history TUI, notifications, remote/synced state, new fetch endpoints (out of scope).
